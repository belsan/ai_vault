# AI Corpus

This is my place for anything I find interesting in terms of AI.

## Background:
I am the CTO of a deeptech AI startup, creating AI agents for chip design. I am interested in AI in general and have been learning about and building agent systems for the past 2 years. Essentially since the advent of agents. The field is changing fast and I had to learn a lot, and am still learning a lot. This place is to keep a curated library - links, papers, topics, etc.
I also want to keep a lecture notes style manuscript - essentially what I think a course about the topics I learn about would look like.


## How do I intend to use it - in no particular order:
* Retaining knowledge: after a year or so I want to be able to look up what I learned, from the vague "I have read something that could be useful"
* Onboarding: employees, master students, interns, might not have knowledge. I want to find the relevant info for them.
* Preparing talks: if I am invited to talk in a summer school, a conference, or even as preparation for a panel discussion: a lot of it can be generated from this corpus
* Preparing grant applications: similar, I often need references, motivating paragraphs, etc
* Parking / handing-off ideas and projects: I can't do everything I think of, but here I can park the ideas. And once it is time to hand it off, it is already ready.

## Style
* Papers should be stored offline as well as with a link, and with meaningful names
* Bibliography 
* Notes in a somewhat academic style. In the form of lecture notes, a PhD thesis. A bit of text, properly cited assumptions. LaTex for a refined manuscript, markdown for everything else. 
* Bibliography / Index for all the resources, with short summary descriptions.

## Structure
README.md this file
manuscript/ for the manuscript notes, with a folder for each topic/chapter
resources/ for the raw .pdf files, .md versions of online articles
projects/ for project ideas
inbox/ where I put things to be added to the collection

## Inbox
The inbox free form, more like a GTD inbox. Possible that stuff shows up multiple times. Keep it clean by moving things that were processed to inbox/processed

## Interests
I am specifically interested in
* AI agent systems and harnesses (LLM+toolcalls, etc.)
* Impact of AI on the future of engineering professions, SW and HW
* Estimates on impact of AI on workload, etc.
* AI failures - real world cases where AI systems mess up
* AI and chip design / RTL design. Obviously, this is what we do at our startup.
